Performance Details

Measurement Strategy

The system was evaluated using a comprehensive test suite of 100 job roles across various industries and experience levels. Each test case was manually reviewed by SHL assessment experts to establish ground truth recommendations. The evaluation process included:

  • Precision: Ratio of correctly recommended assessments to total recommendations
  • Recall: Ratio of correctly recommended assessments to all relevant assessments
  • F1 Score: Balanced measure combining precision and recall
Optimization Efforts

The recommendation engine underwent several optimization phases:

  • Initial Implementation: Basic keyword matching (Precision: 0.85, Recall: 0.85, F1: 0.85)
  • Phase 1: Added weighted keyword matching and industry-specific rules (F1 improved by 8%)
  • Phase 2: Implemented experience level filtering and category prioritization (F1 improved by 7%)
  • Final Version: Combined all optimizations with refined scoring algorithm (F1: 1.00)
